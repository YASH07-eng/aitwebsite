Ahmedbad Institute of Technology provides 7 UG Programs and 3 PG Programs for Engineering and MBA. Details are provided under

Automobile Engineering (B.E.)

Recent trend need high Skill Automobile Engineer Ahmedabad Institute Of Technology’s Department of Automobile Engineering in 2016 which offer “Bachelor of Engineering”. Department is having all Teaching Faculty members, minimum Master Degree holder as well highly professional and Academic Experience. The department has active students chapters of professional bodies like SAE. Student have good opportunities in government Sector Like RTO as well outside India.

Civil Engineering(B.E.)

Since 2012, the Civil Engineering Department at AIT has been producing quality technical manpower needed by industry, R&D organizations , and academic institutions. The Department 's academic activities emphasize understanding of fundamental principles, developing creative ability to handle Civil Engineering challenges and analytical ability to solve interdisciplinary problems. The Department also encourages its students to participate in extracurricular and co-curricular activities, which are necessary for growth, team spirit fostering and organizational skills development. The department not only ensures that the courses are updated but also reflects the current needs of trade , industry and society as a whole.

Computer Engineering(B.E.)

Computer engineering is an evolving branch,which produces incredible advancements over time which revolves around networking,programming,web development,hardware programming. machine learning,artificial intelligence,image processing etc. The Computer Department of Ahmedabad Institute of Technology is quite enthusiastic to be up to date with the recent trends in superior technologies.We not only provide theoretical knowledge about the subject,but also we enhance the student’s learning skills by giving them practical hands on experience in different programming languages. The major subjects of this branch focuses on designing,development,management,algorithms and programming languages.

Information Engineering(B.E.)

The Information technology branch is the pioneer branch for all the technical fields.IT branch offers a variety of subjects focusing on root level installation ,implementing the tools and algorithms,designing and debugging the computer system as well as applications. The IT department of Ahmedabad Institute of Technology offers core subjects along with elective subjects to complement the career advancement in software as well as hardware engineering. The major areas of information technology focuses on database management, networking, security, data mining, cyber security, technical compatibilities along with creative innovations to eliminate the research gap.

Electronic and Communication Engineering(B.E.)

Electronics and Communication Engineering is a leading branch of engineering. Every recent trend and new development consists of something that finds its roots in electronics and communication. Electronics and Communications Engineering involves researching, designing, developing and testing of electronic equipment used in various systems. Electronics and Communications engineers also conceptualize and oversee the manufacturing of communications and broadcast systems. It is a Branch of Engineering in which you can learn both hardware and software as well as there are many job opportunities in government sector and private sector.

Mechanical Engineering (B.E.)

One of the oldest and broadest engineering branch, Mechanical Engineering involves designing, development, production and use of mechanical tools to solve mechanical issues. Generally hired by manufacturing industries, these engineers have employment opportunity in both private and public sectors. Government sectors like Defense, PWD, Post and Telegraphs, and CPWD also hire mechanical engineers. Ahmedabad Institute Of Technology’s Department of Mechanical Engineering was established in 2004, which offer “Bachelor of Engineering”. The department runs one post graduate “Master of Engineering in” since 2012 under Gujarat Technological University. In house We Have Lots of Club like ISHARE , CADCLUB which Makes the student industry Ready.
